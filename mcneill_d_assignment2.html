Saxophone My Main Hobby

Middle School: Learning the Basics

When I was only in the 5th grade I made the decision to join the band program at my middle school. This included learning a new instrument and becoming somewhat proficient at it for concerts and other performances. Without putting very much thought into it I decided that I wanted to play the Saxophone. Since that day playing saxophone has become my favorite hobby and I have participated in many different and prestigious bands .

HighSchool: Perfecting the Technique

As i got older and progressed into highschool I decided to continue playing saxophone. Here I began to learn jazz and began to participate in marching band. This is where I started to realize that I was well above average in terms of technique and overall skill at saxophone. As a freshman I was named first chair and principle Alto Saxophone. As I progressed through my 4 years of highschool the jazz band I was a part of went to numerous competitions where we usually placed near the top or even won. I was even selected to be a part of University of North Carolina at Pembroke's Jazz honor band, featuring a guest artist Kenny Holman.

College: Now

Fast forward 4 years and I am now in college,I still participate in jazz and marching band here at East Carolina University. Although I cannot devote the time to playing saxophone that I once could I still manage to enjoy it.
