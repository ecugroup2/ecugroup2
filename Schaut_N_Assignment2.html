Nick's Schaut's Running History

Background

In elementary and middle schools, I was bullied heavily. I never really fit into any of the popular groups and I never enjoyed any of the things they did. I just couldn't fit in. As a result, they picked on me and teased me heavily. I would get into fights often and at least once a year, I was sent to the principle's office. All of this bullying made me really angry and sad. I needed something to lift me up and make me happier. Drugs and alcohol were out of the question, so one of my counselors suggested that I go for a run...

Middle School

As my stress and anger grew, I reached my breaking point. I had to do something. So I joined the cross country team and decided to try running competitively. I was suprised to find out that I really enjoyed it. Even though the races I ran in were only 2.5 miles, and I wasn't the best runner out there, I knew I had found my sport. I continued to work hard at both school and running, and eventually, I was one of the best on my team by the 8th grade.

High School

In high school, I immediatly tried out and made the cross country team. I found this was a great way to relieve the stress of school as well as make lots of new friends. Every day our team would run varying distances; soome days we would only run a few miles, other days we would run between 4 and 6 miles and othe days we would run close to 10 or 12 miles. Our coach was an IronMan and as a result of this, he expected a lot out of us. The work we put in made us one of the best teams out there. All the work I put in helped make me one of the best on my team.
